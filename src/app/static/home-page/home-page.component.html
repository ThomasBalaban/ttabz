The Beginning

My journey into web development started at a young age when I was first getting involved in competitive gaming. As a player of strategy games back then like Runescape and Yugioh I soon found myself involved in clans and sites thats often needed updates and/or improvements. I got well versed in Adobe Photoshop and started making websites in Freewebs and Geocities, have you heard of these (sorry if you have)? I started making or updating websites for my groups and various other small but unique organizations such as Alter Reality Games. By the time I reached High School I was already committed to being a Web Developer.

I attended Pittsburgh Technical College and studied under Josh Sager and Frank Schurter. During my time there I became a teachers assistant due to my previous experience and teaching nature! I always enjoyed helping lead others to their path of success, and tutoring was a great real world experience to help me learn how to become better at that along with learning a lot myself! You would be surprised what kind of bugs can come out of the same small project from different points of view.
